## P0 — Ship-blockers (fix today)

### The Lean Stack OOS — no back-in-stock capture — `/products/the-lean-stack`
**Issue:** Product JSON-LD reports `availability: OutOfStock`. ATC form renders but cannot convert. No Klaviyo BIS popup, no email capture, no redirect.
**Evidence:** JSON-LD `"availability": "https://schema.org/OutOfStock"`, price $76.45, AggregateRating missing from schema.
**Impact:** Every session on this PDP ($76.45 AOV) exits with zero conversion path. At estimated 50–100 daily sessions, that's $3,800–$7,600/day in lost pipeline.
**Fix:** (1) Activate Klaviyo Back-in-Stock flow on this PDP (10 min in Klaviyo). (2) Add "Notify Me" button via theme or Klaviyo embed. (3) If restock ETA > 7 days, 301 to `/products/the-mojo-shred-stack` ($99.96) as closest alternative.
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

## P1 — High-impact fixes (this week)

### Meta descriptions missing sitewide — all pages
**Issue:** 0 out of 93 crawled pages have a `<meta name="description">` tag. Every SERP snippet is auto-generated by Google.
**Evidence:** WebFetch extraction returned no meta description on any Tier 1, Tier 2, or Tier 3 sample page.
**Impact:** CTR loss of 5–15% on branded and non-branded queries. Google may pull irrelevant snippet text.
**Fix:** Populate Shopify SEO description fields on all Tier 1 PDPs first (13 products + homepage = 1 hour of copy work), then collections, then pages. No dev needed — Shopify admin only.
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

### OG tags missing sitewide — all pages
**Issue:** No `og:title`, `og:description`, `og:image` confirmed on any page. Social shares render with no preview card.
**Evidence:** WebFetch did not surface any OG meta tags across 93 pages. May be JS-injected — verify via Facebook Sharing Debugger.
**Impact:** Social share CTR drops 30–50% without preview cards. Affects paid social ad link previews.
**Fix:** Verify in browser page source (`Ctrl+U`). If truly missing, add OG tags in `theme.liquid` `<head>` using Shopify's `{{ page_title }}`, `{{ page_description }}`, and `{{ product.featured_image }}` objects.
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

### H1 tags missing on 22/24 Tier 1 pages
**Issue:** Only `/pages/programs` has a semantic `<h1>`. PDPs, collections, homepage, and trust pages lack H1s entirely.
**Evidence:** WebFetch H1 count = 0 on 22 of 24 Tier 1 pages. `/products/kino-shred` has an H1 but it's a descriptive sentence, not the product title.
**Impact:** Googlebot uses H1 as a primary ranking signal. Missing H1 weakens topical relevance for product and category queries.
**Fix:** In `product.liquid`, ensure `<h1>{{ product.title }}</h1>` renders server-side. In `collection.liquid`, add `<h1>{{ collection.title }}</h1>`. One theme edit, all pages fixed.
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

### `/products/test` indexed in sitemap
**Issue:** Literal test product handle `test` is live at `/products/test` and included in `sitemap_products_1.xml`.
**Evidence:** Sitemap crawl found URL; page returns 200 OK.
**Impact:** Damages brand credibility if surfaced in search. Potential for accidental orders if priced.
**Fix:** Draft (unpublish) the product immediately in Shopify admin. It will auto-remove from the sitemap.
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

### Dual review widgets on Kino Nitro — `/products/kino-nitro`
**Issue:** Both Okendo (4.9★ / 406 reviews) and Yotpo (4.5★ / 86 reviews) render on this PDP. Conflicting star ratings confuse shoppers and dilute social proof.
**Evidence:** WebFetch detected both `okendo` and `yotpo` widget configurations in page JS.
**Impact:** Split review display undermines trust. Google may pull the lower 4.5★ rating into rich snippets.
**Fix:** Remove Yotpo script/embed from this PDP. Migrate the 86 Yotpo reviews into Okendo via CSV import to consolidate.
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

### Staging label in production title — `/pages/mojo-mastery-quiz`
**Issue:** Title tag reads "Mojo Mastery Challenge Quiz - Updated" — the "- Updated" suffix is a staging/internal label leaked to production.
**Evidence:** WebFetch title extraction.
**Impact:** Visible in SERPs and browser tabs. Looks unprofessional; may confuse users.
**Fix:** Remove "- Updated" from the page title in Shopify admin.
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

### Kino Sleep & Kino Probiotic — critically low review counts (8 each)
**Issue:** Both PDPs show only 8 reviews each (vs. catalog average of 200–800). Social proof is effectively absent.
**Evidence:** JSON-LD AggregateRating: Kino Sleep 5.0/8, Kino Probiotic 4.8/8.
**Impact:** Low review count suppresses conversion rate by 15–25% vs. well-reviewed SKUs. Also weakens Product rich snippet competitiveness.
**Fix:** Launch targeted Okendo review-request flow for all Kino Sleep and Kino Probiotic buyers in the last 90 days. Consider seeding with a post-purchase incentive (loyalty points).
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

### `/cart` is indexable — no noindex directive
**Issue:** The cart page at `/cart` returns 200 with no `<meta name="robots" content="noindex">` detected.
**Evidence:** WebFetch crawl returned full page content with no robots meta.
**Impact:** Cart page indexed in Google wastes crawl budget and can surface in SERPs (bad UX for searchers landing on empty cart).
**Fix:** Add `{% if template == 'cart' %}<meta name="robots" content="noindex, follow">{% endif %}` in `theme.liquid`.
**First seen:** 2026-04-13.

## P2 — Opportunities (this sprint / backlog)

### No semantic JSON-LD on collections or custom pages (44/45 Tier 2 pages)
**Issue:** Only `/pages/clearance-items` (native Shopify template) has schema.org types. All Zipify/PageFly-built pages lack Product, Organization, FAQPage, BreadcrumbList, or CollectionPage schemas.
**Fix:** Inject JSON-LD via custom liquid snippets or a Shopify SEO app. Prioritize collection pages and FAQ page.

### All images served as JPG/PNG — no WebP/AVIF
**Issue:** Across all PDPs and pages, images are served in legacy formats only.
**Fix:** Shopify CDN supports automatic WebP via `| image_url` filter with `format: 'webp'`. Single theme edit saves 25–40% image weight sitewide.

### 23+ orphaned/legacy URLs indexed in sitemaps
**Issue:** Multiple `copy-of-*`, `*-dup`, BFCM 2019, and internal-use URLs are live and in the sitemap.
**Evidence:** `/products/copy-of-lazy-ripped-guys-mojo-nitro-collagen-octane`, `/pages/copy-of-about-kinobody`, `/pages/copy-of-contact-us-2`, `/pages/masterclass-dup`, `/pages/kinobody-homepage-2020`, `/pages/webinar-2023`, `/pages/aggressive-fat-loss-1`, `/pages/aggressive-fat-loss-2`, `/pages/stacks-1`, `/pages/contact-us` (dup of `/pages/contact`), 7× BFCM 2019 collections, `/collections/facebook-catalog-feed`, `/collections/discount_lambo`, `/collections/vipclients`, `/collections/quiz-collection`, `/collections/zaymo-collection`.
**Fix:** Unpublish or 301-redirect each to canonical equivalent. Priority: unpublish `/products/test`, 301 `copy-of-*` and `*-dup` pages, noindex internal collections.

### Console.log debug code in production
**Issue:** `console.log()` statements found on `/pages/refer-a-friend`, `/pages/newsletter-sign-up`, and `/pages/kino-mojo`.
**Fix:** Remove debug logging from production theme JS.

### Duplicate bundle URLs — `/pages/gg-msb-bundle` and `/pages/msb-gg-bundle`
**Issue:** Both URLs resolve to "Movie Star Body & Greek God Bundle" with identical content.
**Fix:** 301-redirect one to the other.

### Duplicate product URL — `/products/lean-stack` and `/products/the-lean-stack`
**Issue:** Both exist in the sitemap. Only `/products/the-lean-stack` is Tier 1 monitored.
**Fix:** 301-redirect `/products/lean-stack` → `/products/the-lean-stack`.

### Negative inventory values on multiple PDPs
**Issue:** Kino Mojo (-24,455), Kino Nitro (-6,131), Kino Shred (-7,675), Aminos (-1,742), Kino Probiotic (-2,730), Mojo Stack Plus Octane (-535), Mojo Shred Stack (-708).
**Fix:** Set to "Don't track inventory" or run inventory adjustment. Negative inventory can cause unexpected OOS if tracking is re-enabled.

### SKU field issues in Product JSON-LD (5/14 PDPs)
**Issue:** 5 PDPs use Shopify product IDs or MPN instead of merchant SKUs. 2 have no SKU at all.
**Fix:** Populate the SKU field in Shopify admin for each variant.

### Internal collections publicly indexed
**Issue:** `/collections/facebook-catalog-feed`, `/collections/discount_lambo`, `/collections/vipclients` expose internal campaign names.
**Fix:** Unpublish from online sales channel or noindex.

### BFCM 2019 collections still indexed (7 URLs)
**Issue:** 7 Black Friday 2019 collections still live and indexed — 6+ years stale.
**Fix:** Unpublish all 7 from online sales channel.

### `/pages/high-t-fat-loss-private` publicly indexed
**Issue:** URL contains "private" but is in the sitemap and returns 200.
**Fix:** Verify intent. If private, unpublish or password-protect.

### Blog nearly absent — only 2 posts indexed
**Issue:** `sitemap_blogs_1.xml` contains only 1 blog index + 2 posts. Content marketing has no SEO footprint.
**Fix:** Audit content calendar if blog is part of growth strategy.
