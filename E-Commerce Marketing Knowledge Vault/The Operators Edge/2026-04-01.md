## Deep Dive: Pinterest — The High-Intent Shopping Channel Most DTC Brands Ignore

Pinterest's commercial advantage is structural: **600 million monthly users** arrive in discovery mode — not doom-scrolling, not watching creators, but actively searching for their next purchase. The platform's own data shows **96% of top search results are unbranded**, which means shoppers haven't chosen a brand yet when they land on a category query. That's the opposite of Google, where branded results dominate. For DTC brands in fashion, home, beauty, and wellness, this is a wide-open shelf.

The channel's purchase intent numbers back this up. [Pinterest's own research](https://business.pinterest.com/blog/how-to-set-up-shopping-ads-on-pinterest/) shows merchants with Catalogs connected earn **5x more impressions** than those without, and product feeds with **2,500+ SKUs see 3x higher ROAS** as Pinterest's ML has more inventory to optimize against. The mechanics are straightforward via Shopify: connect your Catalogs, enable Shopping Ads, and let the feed run. Shoppable Pins display price, availability, and a direct link — collapsing discovery to product page in one tap.

The new performance lever is **Pinterest Performance+**, launched in full in Q1 2026. [Per the official Pinterest Business blog](https://business.pinterest.com/blog/pinterest-performance-plus-campaigns/), Performance+ bundles AI-powered automation — creative testing, audience targeting, and placement selection — into a single campaign type. Internal tests show **10%+ improvement in CPC and CPA** versus traditional campaign setups. It runs like Meta's Advantage+ but against Pinterest's uniquely high-intent search graph. Separately, [Top of Search Ads](https://business.pinterest.com/blog/show-up-first-with-pinterest-top-of-search-ads/) — which guarantee position 1 on relevant category queries — drove **45% higher CTR and 53% higher ROAS** for JCPenney versus standard Consideration campaigns. On a platform where 45% of clicks happen in the top 10 results, owning slot #1 on "bedroom refresh ideas" or "protein powder" is material.

> [!tip] Takeaway
> Immediately: connect your Shopify product catalog to Pinterest via the Pinterest app, upload your full SKU list, and enable Shopping Ads. Then test one Performance+ campaign on your 2–3 hero categories — the AI optimization needs volume to work, so don't start with a niche subcategory. Pinterest trends also last [2x longer than on other platforms](https://business.pinterest.com/blog/pinterest-brand-moments-guide-2026/), so content built around Pinterest's 2026 trend report has a longer shelf life than on TikTok or Instagram.

## Deep Dive: CTV Shoppable Ads — From Brand Awareness to Real ROAS

Connected TV is no longer a brand-awareness line item you include to make TV buyers feel relevant. It is now a measurable performance channel with ROAS data, conversion attribution, and first-party audience targeting — and the case studies are coming in from DTC brands, not just Fortune 500s.

The structural tailwind: **$38 billion** in US CTV ad spend is projected for 2026, and **87% of streaming viewers watch with a second screen in hand**, with [65% using it to visit an advertiser's site mid-stream](https://mountain.com/blog/ctv-shoppable-ads/). That dual-screen behavior is the unlock — a 30-second CTV spot can drive a qualified direct site visit in real time. MNTN's Performance TV platform, which positions CTV as a direct-response channel, has published DTC benchmarks that are hard to dismiss: **Princess Polly exceeded its ROAS goal by 305%**, **Kane Footwear delivered 250% higher ROAS than goal**, and **Beddy's exceeded its holiday ROAS goal by 150%** — all first-time TV advertisers, which is true of [90% of MNTN's client base](https://mountain.com/blog/real-world-performance-wins-from-mntns-top-retail-advertisers/).

The halo effect is the underappreciated angle. MNTN's cross-channel data shows that brands running CTV campaigns see **22% stronger conversion rates for paid search and 9% stronger for paid social** in parallel — the TV impression elevates downstream intent across the whole funnel. For DTC brands doing $5M–$50M in revenue with active Google and Meta programs, CTV can function as a full-funnel multiplier rather than a standalone bet. The tactical entry point: [MNTN x Triple Whale integration](https://www.triplewhale.com/blog/mntn-x-triple-whale) pulls CTV impression and conversion data directly into Triple Whale dashboards, so DTC operators can measure CTV performance in the same MER framework they use for Meta and Google — no separate attribution model required.

A real-world creative tactic: [Blue Wheel ran an Amazon Sponsored TV campaign for Jack Black](https://www.bluewheelmedia.com/blog/connected-tv-ads-for-ecommerce-brands) targeting Thursday Night Football viewers, with a QR code driving to a DTC landing page with a unique coupon code. The QR format allows bottom-of-funnel CTV measurement: scan = attribution. It's now available on Roku, Amazon Fire TV, and select Hulu/Disney+ inventory.

> [!example] Entry Playbook
> 1. Start with a **retargeting campaign** on MNTN or Amazon Streaming TV — target your site visitors and email list on CTV. Lower spend, faster feedback loop.
> 2. Add a **QR code overlay** on all creatives pointing to a dedicated landing page with a unique discount code (5–10% off is enough to drive scans).
> 3. Connect MNTN to Triple Whale to measure CTV ROAS alongside Meta and Google in one dashboard before scaling.

## Quick Headlines

**Meta/Instagram Shops:** Meta quietly ended native in-app checkout on Instagram and Facebook Shops in **September 2025** — all "Buy" clicks now route to your external website. The storefront and product tags remain, but Instagram is now a discovery layer, not a closed checkout. At Shoptalk 2026, [Meta announced it will test AI-generated product summaries and user review highlights](https://techcrunch.com/2026/03/25/meta-turns-to-ai-to-make-shopping-easier-on-instagram-and-facebook/) appearing after ad and website clicks — similar to Amazon's generative AI review feature. Instagram Reels creators are also getting direct access to brand product catalogs in **22 countries** for affiliate tagging. Net: Instagram is doubling down on discovery and creator commerce while ceding the checkout transaction to your owned site.
